60s Pitch

Intro

A funding system that empowers organizations by seeking benefit over profit.

To let organizations grow, next to "profit" and "sales" figures, we need to start counting details like, how much they care about the environment, working conditions for their employees or whether they use open source license to share their knowledge.

How it works?

  1. Define categories for organizations to determine the benefits
  2. Rate each item in the category
  3. Distribute the fund periodically to all organizations based on their overall performance

Results (14 Feb. '17)

As an example, we prepared a demo survey for License Category, and asked people to rate each license based on which one would be more helpful to reaching to Development Goals.

Here are the results:

  • Number of participants: 6
  • Apache-2.0: 27,8%
  • EULA: 17,4 %
  • GPL-3.0: 27,8 %
  • MIT: 27,0 %
